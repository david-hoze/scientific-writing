# Analysis

## Structural entropy ratio

**sigma(s,d)** = |U(s,d)| / M(s,d)

Where:
- `U(s,d)` = restriction universe (union of all restriction images)
- `M(s,d)` = max restriction image for any single function/direction

## Top share

**alpha(d)** = M / |U|, the fraction of the universe covered by the largest single image.
